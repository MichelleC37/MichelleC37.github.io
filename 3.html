MY MUSIC TASTE

My music taste connects to my approach as a designer because as I listen to my favourite artists they form a balance between emotion, creativity, and storytelling. Listening to artists like The Weeknd, Ariana Grande, and Taylor Swift, I am drawn to the way that they approach their music, creating moods, textures, and narratives within their songs, which are qualities that I try to translate into my design work. The Weeknd approaches his work to illustrate a mood atmosphere with experimenting with depth and contrast. Ariana Grande’s polished and bold pop approach encourages me to reflect on clarity and striking visuals. Taylor Swift’s storytelling and attention to details reminds me of how important it is for design intentionality. Other artists have inspired me in so many other ways, to the way that they produce music, to the final outcome. Ultimately, my music has shaped my design path and encourages me to blend personal experience, structure, and style into my work.
